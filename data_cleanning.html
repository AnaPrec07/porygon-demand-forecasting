Data Processing Executive Summary

Walmart provided sales information for 3,049 unique SKUs accross 64 months (2011-2016) to a total of 1,951,360 observations. The training period (Jan 2011 to Dec 2014) is composed of 146,352 of these observations with 2,987 unique SKUs. Below you can see the filters applied to the data to ensure quality and reliability for the demand forecasting model.

From 145,803 observations, the final dataset is only 55, 803. This is 38.3% of the original data.

Applying Log-Normal Transformation to the Sales Distribution

The graph below shows the distribution before and after the trasnformation.
